About Us

Introduction

Travofast is a global technology company that is built around connecting customers to travel agencies, logistics and payment services. The logistic services enables the delivery of goods through travel companies while payment services enable payment of transport fares with travofast ecosystem.

Background

Travofast origins back to 2021 when Gumisiriza Godfrey called in other members to start this project. The business is growing through the visionary idea of linking customers to travel companies.

Mission

To make it easy to connect with travel companies anywhere at anytime.

Vision

To become the number one online company that link customers to travel agencies around Africa and the rest of the world
